Test Collection Based Evaluation of Information Retrieval Systems: full paper and accompanying slides

Full paper

In 2010, I published an extensive review on IR evaluation, focusing on test collection based evaluation. Here you can find a copy of the full paper; the publishers of Foundations and Trends® in Information Retrieval allow authors to put the full PDF of their paper up on their web site for free. The citation for the paper is

Sanderson, M. (2010). Test Collection Based Evaluation of Information Retrieval Systems. Foundations and Trends® in Information Retrieval, 4(4), 247-375. doi:10.1561/1500000009

Slides

I created a set of slides that largely follow the order of the article. They were presented at the Yahoo! IR Summer School in Bangalore in 2011. They were split into part one (PPT) (PDF) and part two (PPT) (PDF). If you do use some or all of the slides, please reference the paper.

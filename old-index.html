About me

I am Professor at RMIT University. I'm a researcher in information retrieval. It's my privilege to be part of the RMIT IR group. I am or have been a co-editor of Foundations and Trends in Information Retrieval; an Associate Editor of ACM Transactions on the Web, ACM Transactions on Information Systems Information Processing and Management, and IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering; I have also been on the editorial board of Journal of the Association for Information Science and Technology (JASIST) and The Information Retrieval Journal. I am also a visiting professor at NII in Tokyo.

I am a Director of the RMIT Enabling Capability Platform with a focus on IT and parts of Engineering. In that role, I have modified a Google Search to enable search of RMIT staff pages. I am also a Chief Investigator of the ARC Centre of Excellence on Automated Decision Making and Society.

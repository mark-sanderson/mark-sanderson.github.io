Getting to RMIT.

How to get here

Computer Science is on the corner of Swanston Street and Franklin Street in the Central Business District of Melbourne. Near the corner there is an entrance from Swanston Street up an escalator. When you get to the top, turn right 180 degrees and walk to the lifts. Take a lift to the 9th floor, exit and turn left going through a set of glass doors. Once through the doors, turn right and follow the winding corridor past offices, a set of posters, and finally to my office: room number 14.09.17.
